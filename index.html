What AI Lekhani does

Everything a novelist needs.
Nothing a developer built for.

Built specifically for Indian writers — not adapted from a Western tool.

📖
Story Codex
Characters, world rules, timeline, and plot — all stored as structured memory. The AI reads the relevant parts before every response. Never forgets Arjun's age or the letter from Chapter 3.
🌐
True Multilingual
Write in Hindi, Tamil, or English with style modes — literary, mythic, devotional, dramatic. Guard rails prevent translation-smell and wrong sentence structure. Honorifics are preserved automatically.
⚠️
Continuity Engine
After every AI response, AI Lekhani extracts facts and checks them against your accepted canon. Dead characters, age conflicts, impossible location jumps — you get a warning before the inconsistency becomes your problem.
🎤
Voice Input
Speak your scene instructions instead of typing. Say "Chapter 8 — the queen confronts the monk near the river but don't reveal the betrayal yet" and AI Lekhani turns it into a structured writing prompt.
🔒
Your Data, Your Device
Everything saves in your browser. Your writing never touches a server. Export an encrypted .ailekhani backup file — only your password can open it. Restore on any device, any time.
🤖
Any AI Provider
Connect Gemini (free), Claude, or ChatGPT. Switch models mid-session. AI Lekhani auto-selects the best model available on your account. You pay the AI provider directly — AI Lekhani takes nothing.

Build your story world
Fill your Story Codex
Create a book project. Name your characters, describe your world, write your plot outline. This becomes your Story Codex — the memory your AI carries into every chapter. The more detail you add, the more consistent your story stays.
Story Codex example
Character: Arjun Mehta, 32
Role: Disgraced detective
Setting: Bombay, 1947
World rule: No one speaks the King's name aloud
✓ Injected into every AI call
3
Write chapter by chapter
Tell the AI what happens. It writes the scene.
Type an instruction like "Draft the scene where Arjun finds the sealed letter at the harbour" — or just say "Continue" and the AI picks up from where it left off. You can also speak your instructions using the voice button. Review, regenerate, or continue building.
A chapter in progress

4
Stay consistent automatically
AI Lekhani watches your story for contradictions
After every AI response, AI Lekhani extracts the key facts — who appeared, what happened, what was established — and checks them against your accepted story canon. If something contradicts, you see a warning. You decide what's right. Nothing changes without your approval.

Questions

Frequently asked

An API key is like a password that connects AI Lekhani to an AI service like Gemini or Claude. You create it on the AI provider's website — it takes about 90 seconds for Gemini and is completely free. AI Lekhani needs it to send your writing instructions to the AI and receive the written text back. Your key is stored only in your browser and never sent to AI Lekhani's servers.
Your writing is stored entirely in your browser on your own device. AI Lekhani has no server that stores your manuscripts. The only time your text leaves your device is when it's sent to the AI provider (Gemini, Claude, or ChatGPT) to generate the next part of your story — the same way it would if you were using those services directly. You can export an encrypted backup of everything at any time.
Gemini (Google's AI) has a generous free tier — you can write a lot before spending anything. Claude costs approximately ₹2 per writing session. ChatGPT costs approximately ₹3 per session. A "session" is one back-and-forth exchange — so writing a full chapter might cost ₹5–15 depending on how many drafts you generate. AI Lekhani itself is free and takes nothing from these costs.
Yes. AI Lekhani is fully responsive and designed for mobile writing. On a phone, the chapter sidebar becomes a slide-in drawer, the Codex opens as a bottom sheet, and there is a bottom navigation bar for quick access to all features. The voice input feature works on Chrome for Android. Safari on iPhone has limited voice support due to Apple's browser restrictions, but all other features work perfectly.
Each book project has its own writing language setting. You can have one project in Hindi, another in Tamil, and another in English. You can also switch the language of a project at any time in Settings. The AI will write all prose in whichever language you've selected, with the appropriate style and cultural conventions for that language.
Your writing is stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing your browser will delete it — so export a backup regularly using the Backup button in the sidebar. Backups are encrypted with your own password and can be restored on any device or any browser. Cross-device sync (automatic) is coming in AI Lekhani Pro.
