## What is AI Lekhani?

AI Lekhani is a writing tool that helps Indian writers plan, write, and finish their novels using AI. It maintains your Story Codex — characters, locations, timeline, and plot — so your story stays consistent across every chapter.

- Write in **English and Hindi** on the free plan
- Your writing stays in **your browser** — we never store your prose
- Bring your own **Gemini API key** — free from Google
- Export your work as a **plain text file** any time
